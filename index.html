About P. L. Deshpande

Purushottam Laxman Deshpande, popularly known as P. L. Deshpande (Pu. La.), was one of the greatest Marathi writers. He was a humorist, actor, playwright, musician and social worker. His humorous stories and unforgettable characters continue to entertain readers of all ages.

Most Famous Books

Vyakti Ani Valli

A collection of memorable character sketches. It is considered one of P. L. Deshpande's greatest literary works.

Asa Mi Asami

A humorous novel about the life of an ordinary middle-class man, filled with wit and comedy.

Batatyachi Chaal

A classic comedy portraying the funny and heartwarming life of people living in a Mumbai chawl.
